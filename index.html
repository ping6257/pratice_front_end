Recipe

Fruit Series

Raspberry , Strawberry ,Mango triple favor included

Diffculty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

    Black Chocolate:200g

    Sugar : 150g

    Butter : 200g

    Eggs : 4

    Flour : 80g

Instruction

1

Melt the Chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar , eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything to get the smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
